Tensions Mount Between India and Pakistan

Diplomatic relations remain fragile amid ongoing territorial disputes.

The ongoing conflict between India and Pakistan escalated this week following a military incident along the Line of Control. Political leaders from both nations have called for international mediation to de-escalate tensions. Citizens on both sides remain hopeful for a peaceful resolution.
