Hollywood Embraces AI in Filmmaking

Artificial intelligence is transforming the creative process in the film industry.

Major Hollywood studios are integrating AI tools in scriptwriting, visual effects, and marketing. While some critics worry about creativity loss, many celebrate the technology for its efficiency and innovation. This trend is expected to grow in upcoming productions.
